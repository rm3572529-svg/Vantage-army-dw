Common Questions

Why isn't my download working?
Make sure your Google Drive file is set to "Anyone with the link can view." Private files cannot be downloaded via VantageDown.
How do I get more than 25 links?
Complete the social tasks in your dashboard (YouTube, Instagram, WhatsApp) to unlock 50 more links, for a total of 75.
How do I report an inappropriate file?
Use the "Report this file" link on the download page, or email us directly at abuse@vantagedown.com with the link URL.
