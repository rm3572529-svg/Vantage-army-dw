About the Developer

Built with precision.
Designed for people.

VantageDown was created to solve a real problem: sharing large files from Google Drive with a professional, clean experience — without relying on paid file hosting services.

The Problem

Google Drive sharing links are messy, hard to share, and often trigger Google's virus scan page instead of downloading directly. Users needed something cleaner.

The Solution

VantageDown converts any Google Drive link into a professional download page with a direct download button, QR code, file info display, and short shareable URL.
